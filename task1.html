Early life

Sumedh Mudgalkar was born in Nanded, Maharashtra, on November 2, 1996. He is from a Marathi Hindu middle-class household. He is the oldest son of Vasudev and Vasanti Mudgalkar. He also has two younger siblings, Sanket and Samiran Mudgalkar. He has keen interest in dance since his childhood. Sumedh Mudgalkar received his primary schooling at Sinhgad Springdale Public School in Pune. He was an ordinary student, but due to his love for dance, he always participated in school dance competition. After finishing high school, he enrolled in the Maharashtra Institute of Technology in Pune to pursue a degree in Economics, but he was unable to complete his studies. Sumedh Mudgalkar was born on 2 November 1996 (age 25 years; as in 2022) in Pune, Maharashtra, India. He did his graduation in Economics from Maharashtra Institute of Technology. His father’s name is Vasudev Mudgalkar and his mother’s name is Vasanti Mudgalkar. Sumedh has 2 brothers Samiran Mudgalkar and Sanket Mudgalkar.

Career

Dancing He made his dance debut on a Marathi reality show, Dance Maharashtra Dance, in 2012 at the age of 15 and was one of the finalists. He appeared in Dance India Dance (Season 4) in 2013, where he reached the finals, finishing as 3rd runner-up.[3][4] Acting Mudgalkar made his acting debut in Channel V’s Dil Dosti Dance, a dance-based youth show where he portrayed the role of Raghavendra "Raghav" Pratap Singh. Then he became known for portraying the main antagonist Yuvraj Sushim in the Indian historical drama Chakravartin Ashoka Samrat.[5] Sumedh next moved to the Marathi film industry with a cameo in the 2016 film Ventilator as Karan. He then appeared in Manjha, which was his first film as a main antagonist as well as lead actor, in which he portrayed the role of a psychopath named Vicky.[6] In 2018, he played the role of Salil in Bucket List.[7] Recently, he has also been shooting for the Marathi film Mann Yedyagat Zaala.[8] In 2018, he featured in a music video for a Marathi song Bekhabar Kashi Tu.[citation needed] From October 2018 to January 2023, he played the role of Lord Krishna, Lord Vishnu and Lord Rama in Swastik Productions' television series RadhaKrishn on Star Bharat opposite Mallika Singh.[9] In 2021, he reprised his role of Lord Vishnu along with Mallika Singh and Basant Bhatt in RadhaKrishn's prequel series Jai Kanhaiya Lal Ki which also aired on Star Bharat and ended in July 2022. In 2022 he featured as Darkie in Disney+ Hotstar's web series Escaype Live produced and directed by Siddharth Kumar Tewary under Swastik Productions.[10] From May to June 2023, he portrayed the role of Hatim al-Tai opposite Srishti Jain in Sony SAB's Ali Baba.[11] Singing Sumedh made his singing debut in a single, "Mere Paas Tum Raho",[12] in March 2023. The music video featured Sumedh alongside Ambika Devi.

Education

umedh Mudgalkar is one of the charming actors of Indian television. The young actor has swooned over ever since he first appeared on screen and now has a massive fan following of millions all over the country. He first made his debut on screen through the famous show called Dil Dosti Dance and currently winning hearts in Star Bharat show RadhaKrishn where he plays the main lead as Lord Krishna. Now, talking about his education and qualification, Sumedh did his schooling from Sinhgad Springdale Public School, Pune. Later, he did his graduation in Economics from Maharashtra Institute of Technology, Pune. The actor then came to Mumbai to pursue his dreams and become an actor. Now, he is one of the successful actors on the small screen.
